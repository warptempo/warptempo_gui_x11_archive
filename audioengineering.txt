Mozart : Symphony No.40 in G minor, K.550 / Marriner 1970
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-BQ9wbD_qhI

The 1st and 4th mvt. of 550 by Marriner. The whole symphony is in the "Complete Mozart Edition" box set, but most YouTube streams feature a digital recording from the late 80s on EMI. The 1st mvt of the 1970 version is ~7:44 - the later one is ~7:26.
https://music.youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_mOWUWeMX9R7guBXY1BpMXwEcGatwWR41Y

I don't think I ever heard Marriner's version until after I completed most of my work on 550. I mostly listened to this set previously, which uses Marriner's recordings for the early symphonies and Krips's recordings for the late ones. In any case, Marriner's mvts 2 and 3 are completely different, though his 4th mvt is also quite close.
